Standard Cleaning Package

Living Areas & Bedrooms:

  • Basic Dusting: Including all accessible areas, picture frames, and decorative items.
  • Surface Cleaning: Wiping down all tables, chairs, and furniture surfaces. Vacuum Carpets & Rugs
  • Polish Wooden Furniture: Using a high-end, non-toxic polish.
  • Art & Decor Care: Light dusting of art pieces and delicate items with specialized tools.

Kitchen:

  • Countertop Cleaning: Using eco-friendly and food-safe cleaners.
  • Appliance Exterior: Wipe down of stove, fridge, and dishwasher.
  • Sink Scrubbing: With a non-scratch, non-toxic cleaner.
  • Microwave Cleaning: Inside and out.
  • Spot Clean Cabinet Exteriors: Focusing on handles and frequently touched areas.

Bathrooms:

  • Sanitize All Surfaces: Including toilet, sink, and shower.
  • Mirror Polishing: Achieve a streak-free shine for all mirrors.
  • Floor Disinfection: Thoroughly clean and disinfect bathroom floors.
  • Fixture Cleaning: Wipe down all fixtures to maintain cleanliness.
  • Towel and Accessory Care: Ensure towels and accessories are neatly arranged and sanitized.
